<b>Amirsoy Mountain Resort: A Modern Brand of Uzbekistan's Tourism and Global Intersection</b>

<b>Amirsoy Mountain Resort</b> is one of the most modern, fully compliant with international standards, and the largest all-season mountain resorts in Central Asia. This magnificent holiday destination was built in the Bostanlyk district of the Tashkent region, on the slopes of the Maygashkan hills of the Chatkal mountain range. The official opening ceremony of the project took place in December 2019 and within a short period, it brought the tourism potential of our country to a completely new level. Amirsoy has become a favorite place not only for ski sports enthusiasts but also for local and foreign tourists who want to spend quality time in the bosom of nature during all four seasons of the year. The total area of the resort is approximately 900 hectares, and it is located at an altitude of up to 2290 meters above sea level, allowing visitors to enjoy breathtaking mountain landscapes and fresh air.

<b>The resort's infrastructure</b> was designed by the world's leading companies, particularly famous mountain project specialists from Andorra, Austria, and Switzerland, based on international safety and quality requirements. All conditions typical of world-class mountain resorts have been created on the territory of Amirsoy. Modern gondola (closed cabin) and chair lift systems manufactured by the world-famous Austrian company Doppelmayr are installed here to transport passengers safely to the high peaks. These cable cars have the capacity to serve thousands of tourists per hour, carrying resort guests comfortably and quickly to the highest points in a few minutes. Also, there are dozens of ski slopes of various levels of complexity on the mountain slopes, designed for both professionals and beginners.

<b>The skiing and snowboarding</b> season in Amirsoy usually lasts from the beginning of December until the middle of April. In order to ensure the quality and duration of the snow cover, the most advanced artificial snowmaking systems (snow cannons) are installed in the resort, which allows keeping the ski season stable regardless of the whims of the weather. Special snow-grooming vehicles (snowcats) work continuously every night to level the slopes and bring the snow layer to a professional level. For beginners, a professional ski academy operates on the territory of the resort, where international instructors teach both adults and children the secrets of safe movement on the mountain and skiing. Also, rental centers for all types of skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports equipment are at the service of guests.

<b>As a summer and all-season entertainment</b> destination, Amirsoy does not stop its activities during the warm months of the year; on the contrary, it offers active tours in a completely different format. In spring, summer, and autumn, the territory of the resort is covered with greenery, and many services such as mountain biking (downhill), quad biking, a rope town, paintball, trekking, and camping are launched here. Cable cars work continuously in summer as well, offering people the opportunity to escape the scorching Tashkent heat, relax on the cool mountain hills, and capture beautiful nature photos. Special interactive playgrounds and attractions are organized for children, providing all conveniences for family recreation.

<b>The accommodation and service</b> network is an integral part of the resort, consisting of cozy alpine-type cottages (chalets) called "Le Chalet by Amirsoy", built in the style of the European Alps. These cottages form a very beautiful landscape on the mountainside and have fireplaces inside that create a real mountain atmosphere along with modern comforts. Also, high-class restaurants, cafes, and an open-air bar zone offering national and European cuisine operate on the territory. Dining in restaurants located on the highest peak gives a special pleasure while observing the whole valley and snowy mountains. Spa centers, bath complexes, and swimming pools serve to physically restore the body and relax after an active day.

<b>The future of Amirsoy mountain resort</b> and its economic prospects are extremely grand, as within the framework of the second and third phases of the project, it is planned to further expand the territory, build new cable cars and large five-star hotel complexes. This destination plays an important role in increasing the investment attractiveness of Uzbekistan and presenting the country on the world tourism map as an active winter and summer sports center. Due to its compliance with the standards of the International Ski Federation, Amirsoy has the opportunity to host prestigious international sports competitions and championships in the future. In conclusion, Amirsoy mountain resort is a bright symbol of modern development, natural beauty, and high-level hospitality culture of Uzbekistan.
